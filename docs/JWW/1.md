---
title: 高斯积分常用公式
date: 2021-09-19
---


$$
\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-x^2}dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}, \\
\int_{-\infin}^{+\infin}e^{-x^2}dx = \sqrt{\pi}, \\
\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-\frac{x^2}{2}}dx = \int_0^{+\infin}e^{-y^2}d\sqrt{2}y = \frac{\sqrt{2\pi}}{2}, \\
\int_{-\infin}^{+\infin}e^{-\frac{x^2}{2}}dx = \sqrt{2\pi}.
$$
